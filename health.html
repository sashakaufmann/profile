Health

Want to know how to live and eat healthily? So called science is constantly changing. The most robust, tried and tested information is in plain sight. Look at our ancestors. Look at what works.

Meat used to be a rare treat. We worked hard for it and when we got it we gorged. Now meat is on tap. It’s overnutritioning us. If you overfeed a plant, it hurts it. I think we’re the same. Eat meat infrequently. When you do, earn it like a lion earns an antelope. Do lots of physical activity before or after. When you do, eat without carbs.

Our ancesters mostly had access to wild plants. These would have been eaten constantly. Do the same. Eat mostly vegetables and legumes. Eat lots every day.

I love bread. But ground up wheat and other seed gives us too much goodness at once. How difficult would it have been to intake that much grain without processing it? If you eat bread, work hard for it and try to avoid eating it with meat.

Eat fruit whole, don’t drink fruit juice. It’s all of the sugar with none of the fibre.

Drink water. Drink red wine. Drink beer - Germany has a purity law, craft beers and ales are also good quality.
