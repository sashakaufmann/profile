The big ideas

  • Happiness in this life can be achieved by focusing on the natural and essential (which everything we truly need in life is - eg. food, water); and meaningful friendships.
  • To avoid extravagance and reduce desire is to pursue simplicity and what is natural. Just as the subtraction of evil leaves only the good. Pursuit of wealth and doing wrong lead only to fear and suffering.
  • Live simply. Seek and provide friendship. Enjoy the present. Welcome tomorrow but do not seek it.
