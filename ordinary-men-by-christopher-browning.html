Ordinary Men - Reserve Police Battalion 101 and the Final Solution in Poland by Christopher Browning

Date finished 16 Oct 2021

Recommendation: 10/10

It was hard but everyone should read this. Browning discusses many factors that influenced a unit of average middle-aged Germans in becoming cold-blooded murderers of tens of thousands of Jews; obedience to authority, conformity, self justifications and dehumanisation among others. His point is that in certain situations ordinary men are capable of horrific acts that are unthinkable in other circumstances.
